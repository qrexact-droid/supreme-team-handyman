Flooring Types We Install

  • Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP) — the most popular choice in Vegas right now, durable and moisture-resistant
  • Laminate — cost-effective, installs fast, looks clean in any room
  • Ceramic and porcelain tile — kitchens, bathrooms, entryways, and commercial spaces

  • Subfloor repair and leveling — before any flooring goes down
  • Transition strips and trim installation — door thresholds, T-molding, reducers

Why Subfloor Prep Matters

In Las Vegas, slabs are common — and they're rarely perfectly flat. A floor laid on a lumpy or uneven subfloor will creak, click, and start to separate at the seams within a year. We check the subfloor before anything goes down. High spots get ground flat, low spots get filled, and the surface gets clean and dry. That's what makes flooring last.

If your existing floor has old adhesive, tile, or damaged underlayment, we assess that too and let you know what needs to happen before the new material can go in. No surprises after the job starts.

What to Expect

  1. We look at the space, the subfloor condition, and your material — then give you a clear price.
  2. We move any furniture that needs to go (coordinate with us when booking).
  3. We prep the subfloor — level, clean, and dry.
  4. We lay the flooring according to manufacturer specs and good trade practice.
  5. We install all transitions, cut pieces around doorways and fixtures cleanly.
  6. We sweep up, remove debris, and walk through the finished job with you.
