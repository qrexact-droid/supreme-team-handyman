  • Drywall installation for remodels and new walls

Why Las Vegas Homes Crack

Our desert climate is hard on drywall. Extreme summer heat and big day-to-night temperature swings make walls expand and contract, which opens cracks at stress points — especially around windows and over doorways. Foundation settling in newer Vegas developments adds to it. We don't just patch the surface; we fix it so it holds.

Our Repair Process

  1. We inspect the damage and give you a clear, upfront price — no surprises.
  2. We cut out damaged material and patch with new drywall where needed.
  3. We tape, mud, sand, and texture-match to blend with your existing wall.
  4. We prime so the area is paint-ready (ask us about painting too).
  5. We clean up completely before we leave.
