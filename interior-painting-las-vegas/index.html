Why Prep Matters in Las Vegas

Las Vegas heat puts stress on paint. Walls that aren't properly prepped — sanded, patched, and primed — tend to show brush marks, peel at edges, or absorb unevenly in our dry air. We don't skip steps. Every job gets the prep work it needs so the finish looks right and actually holds up over time.

If your walls have cracks, nail pops, or old texture that's falling apart, we can repair those first and paint right after in the same visit. That's one call, one crew, one trip.

Our Painting Process

  1. We assess the surfaces — walls, ceilings, trim — and confirm scope and price upfront.
  2. We protect floors, furniture, and fixtures with drop cloths and painter's tape.
  3. We patch any holes or cracks and sand smooth before any paint goes on.
  4. We prime where needed — especially on patches, new drywall, or color changes.
  5. We apply paint with clean, even technique — no roller marks, no drips.
  6. We pull tape, do a final walk-through, and clean up completely.
