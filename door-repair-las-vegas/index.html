Door Problems We Fix

Most door issues in Las Vegas come down to heat expansion, frame settling, or plain old wear. We handle all of it:

  • Doors that stick, drag, or won't latch
  • Broken or bent hinges
  • Stripped hinge screws and loose hardware
  • Damaged door frames and jambs
  • Doors that swing open or won't stay closed
  • Deadbolt and lockset installation
  • Weather stripping replacement
  • New interior door installation (prehung or slab)
  • Closet door repair — sliding, bi-fold, and bypass

Why Las Vegas Doors Go Wrong

Heat is the main culprit. In summer, temperatures in Las Vegas regularly hit 110°F and above. Wood doors — and even metal door frames — expand significantly in that heat and contract again at night. Over time that constant movement causes sticking, warping, and loose hardware. Foundation settling, common in newer Las Vegas developments built on desert soil, shifts door frames out of square too.

We diagnose the actual cause before we fix it. If a door is sticking because the frame is out of square, planing the door edge alone won't last — we correct the root problem.

New Door Installation

Need a new door hung? We install prehung interior doors, slab doors, and closet doors. We handle the whole job — removing the old door, fitting the new one, shimming and securing the frame, installing hardware, and trimming out. When we leave, the door swings smooth and closes flush every time.

We don't sell doors, but we'll work with whatever you purchase from Home Depot, Lowe's, or a supplier. If you need guidance on what to buy, we're happy to advise over the phone before you shop.
