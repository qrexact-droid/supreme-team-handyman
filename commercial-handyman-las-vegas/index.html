Commercial Services We Provide

We handle the full range of handyman maintenance for commercial and multi-family properties:

  • Drywall repair and patch between tenants
  • Interior painting — units, common areas, hallways, stairwells
  • Door repair and replacement — commercial grade hardware, closers, locks
  • Flooring repair and installation — LVP, tile, carpet transitions
  • Faucet and fixture replacement in commercial restrooms and kitchens
  • Garbage disposal replacement in multi-family units
  • Light fixture and ceiling fan swaps
  • Caulking and weatherstripping — exterior doors, windows
  • General punch-list work for unit turnovers
  • Recurring maintenance — weekly, monthly, or on-call

Why Property Managers Call Us

Most commercial property managers in Las Vegas deal with the same frustration: they call a handyman, they wait, the guy shows up late, does halfway-decent work, and disappears when it's time to clean up. That's not how we operate.

We're available 7 days a week, 7am–8pm. We give you an accurate price before the job starts. We communicate clearly about timing and scope. If something changes mid-job, we tell you before we do it. And when the job is done, the space looks like we were never there — except the problem is fixed.

For property managers handling multiple units, we can batch work efficiently — run through several units in sequence and cut your per-unit cost. Call to discuss how we can structure recurring service for your property.

Unit Turnovers and Punch Lists

Tenant turnover is one of the highest-pressure moments for any property manager — you need the unit ready fast, and every day it sits vacant costs money. We specialize in efficient turnovers: drywall patched, walls repainted, fixtures replaced, doors working, and the unit clean and presentable for the next tenant. We can handle the whole punch list in one visit for most units.

Call us as soon as a tenant gives notice and we'll schedule the work so it's done before the lease ends. No scrambling at the last minute.

Commercial Building Maintenance

Ongoing maintenance is what keeps a property looking professional and retaining tenants. We can set up recurring service — monthly walkthroughs, on-call response, or scheduled maintenance visits — so you're not reacting to problems after they've gotten expensive. Talk to us about what your property needs and we'll put together a plan.
